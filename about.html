About Us

Bilqis Coffee stands as one of the leading brands celebrating Yemeni specialty coffee, dedicated to supporting the Yemeni community amidst ongoing economic challenges. Central to our mission is the empowerment of women and farmers involved in coffee cultivation.

Our Journey

Bilqis was established by a group of youth and women in November 2017 as a community initiative to improve the quality of Yemeni coffee and support coffee farmers.

In March 2024, Bilqis opened its first coffee shop in New York, becoming the first Bilqis location to serve Yemeni specialty coffee in the United States. Bilqis maintains a full supply chain — sourcing the finest Yemeni coffee beans directly from farms in Yemen, sorting them at their lab in Yemen, and brewing them fresh for customers in Brooklyn.
